All roles have two types of competencies The first are called "professional competencies," these are the core skills and behaviours that are required for all roles. in addition to this, each individual role has its own role based competencies which are unique to that particular role. The numbers next to the competency represent the mastery level required; "1" signifies Master level and "5" signifies fundamental.
